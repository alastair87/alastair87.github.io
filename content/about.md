I'm Alastair B. Cooper. This site is a space for writing, projects, and notes on technology, institutions, (dis)ability, culture, and the practical conditions that let people turn thought into usable work.

My interests often sit between systems and lived experience: how tools shape what people can do; how ability is interpreted and defined; how AI can function as cognitive scaffolding; and how complex ideas can be made clearer, more testable, or more useful.
